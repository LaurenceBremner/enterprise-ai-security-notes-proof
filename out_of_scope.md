# Out of Scope

This project is intentionally limited to enterprise integration and security reasoning. It does not implement or claim ownership of the following:

- Kubernetes deployment.
- Helm charts.
- Private cloud or VPC deployment.
- BYOC architecture.
- Production identity-provider integration.
- Production secrets-management implementation.
- Formal SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, PCI, GDPR, or legal compliance certification.
- Penetration testing.
- Multi-region reliability architecture.
- Customer-specific contract negotiation.
- Production enterprise AI platform ownership.

## How To Read This

The notes are a pre-pilot checklist for the questions that need answers before an AI workflow expands: who can access what, what gets logged, where data is retained, which decisions need approval, and what conditions should block rollout.
